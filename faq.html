What would you like to know?
How does simplebotics.io match products?

Glad you asked. We built a custom platform that matches products on a variety of questions. Each product you see is filtered in real-time using multiple categories. Things like price, age group, and subject matter are considered.


What if I couldn’t find the product I’m looking for?

Contact us and we’ll help you find the perfect STEM product.


I'm a teacher. Where do I find curriculum for my classroom?

This depends on the product. If you filter for products by selecting “teacher,” all products will include a link to their curriculum under “learn more.”


Why are all the “buy now” buttons linked to other sites and not simplebotics.io?

We don’t sell products through simplebotics.io. We’re simply a tool for recommending the best products. To make it simple for our users, we link the purchase buttons to Amazon or other online retailers.


What's with your email newsletter?

Our newsletter is a monthly email sent out to subscribers, and it features fun robotics news and activities for parents and teachers. No spam. Ever.


Can I follow you on social media?

You bet! We're on Twitter for now, but we hope to expand to other networks as we continue to grow.


Is there anything else I should know?

Not really. If you’re a company wanting to work with us, contact us here. If you’re into legal jargon, we have terms and privacy pages.
