About

What we do

simplebotics.io is a web app with a simple mission: to make STEM education more accsessible to everyone. There's plethora of tech toys on the market, and it can be tricky picking the right products for your students and kids. We help you discover programmable robots for your kindergarten class, hackable drones for your budding engineer, and so much more.

Looking for grant information, lesson plans, or anything else? Email us!
